About Lumen

Our story

Lumen started as a small camera shop run by working photographers who were tired of upsells and jargon. Today we curate a focused catalogue of cameras, lenses and accessories, and pair every product with honest guidance so you buy the right tool for the work you actually do.

What we do

We sell gear, but we also teach the craft. Alongside the shop you'll find buying guides and tutorials, plus a showcase of images and video captured on the equipment we carry — so you can see results before you commit.

Where to go next

  • Learn – buying guides and step-by-step tutorials.
  • Showcase – gallery, video reel and slider.

Frequently asked questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. Standard and expedited shipping are available to most countries at checkout.

What is your return policy?

Unopened gear can be returned within 30 days for a full refund.

Can I preview experiences without an account?

Yes. Append URL parameters such as ?variant=b, ?show=promo, or ?hide=beta.

Is my browsing personalized?

Adobe Target's at.js requests offers for the mbox regions on each page and applies them. No Adobe Analytics is loaded.
